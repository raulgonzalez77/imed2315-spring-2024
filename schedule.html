Wedding Fashion Show

Participate in our wedding fashion show by volunteering to model some of our dresses and suits, making donations for our RG' Foundation (Old Dresses or Money), or by being an expectator and passing the good word about our services to the community.

How to participate

Regardless of the method of participation, we'd like to get to know more about you. Let us know if you are:

  • Participating in our wedding runway
  • Donating an item
    • Old wedding dress
    • Tuxedos
    • Set of bride's maid dresses (6 mimimum)
  • Donating Money
